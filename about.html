Our Story

Founded by Jay

CyberSentinel was founded by Jay, also known as ExposingWithJay, with a simple but powerful mission: to protect children from online predators. What started as individual investigations has grown into a coordinated team effort.

Growing the Team

Joined by his brother Linden as co-owner, the organization has expanded to include managers, administrators, moderators, and security specialists, all united by the common goal of child protection.

Making an Impact

Through careful investigation and collaboration with law enforcement, CyberSentinel has helped resolve over 147 cases and continues to work tirelessly to make the internet safer for children.
