Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to reports?

We aim to respond to all reports within 24-48 hours. Urgent cases marked as immediate threats are prioritized and reviewed within hours.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when submitting a report. However, providing contact information helps us follow up if we need additional details.

What happens after I submit a report?

Our team reviews all reports, conducts initial investigation, documents evidence, and coordinates with appropriate law enforcement agencies when necessary.

Do you work with law enforcement?

Yes, we maintain relationships with local, state, and federal law enforcement agencies and provide them with documented evidence from our investigations.

How can I protect my child online?

Visit our educational resources, join our Discord community for tips, and maintain open communication with your children about their online activities.

Can I volunteer with CyberSentinel?

We welcome volunteers with various skills. Contact us through the form above with "Volunteer Application" as the subject to learn about current opportunities.
